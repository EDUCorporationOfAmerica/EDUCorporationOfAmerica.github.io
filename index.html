Importance of Subject - Verb agreement

What is subject/ verb agreement? Subject verb agreement simply means the subject and verb must agree with each other. This means both need to be singular or both need to be plural.

For example: The sky (look/ looks )beautiful tonight. Since the subject sky is in singular form, therefore the verb looks should be in singular form as well.

Another example: I am/are a really good cook. The word I is a singular noun(subject) , therefore we needed a singular verb which is am But if I said: We are/am really good cooks. The word We is in plural form therefore the verb are should be in singular form as well

Why is this important? You may sit here and ask yourself: “ Why do I need this? I speak great English.” Well, we are here to continue your “great” English speaking abilities. We believe that subject verb agreement is very important because we have to communicate clearly in the English language. Its very easy to get confused with the English language. Knowing the proper way to place the correct subject and verb tense, you will never have a problem with speaking to your peers and colleagues.

What you will learn in this lesson? You will learn how to use the proper subject and verb agreement in a sentence
